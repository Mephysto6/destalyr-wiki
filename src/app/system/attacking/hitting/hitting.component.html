Toucher

Hitting

Attaque : 1d12 + stat (force ou dextérité selon armes)
Esquive : 1d12 + agilité
Si esquive strictement plus que attaque = esquivé => aucun dégâts
Si attaque est égale ou plus que esquive = touché => lance les dégâts

Attack : 1d12 + stat (strength or dexterity depending on the weapon type)
Evade : 1d12 + agility
If the evade score is higher than the attack score, then the attack misses and the attack deals no damage.
If the attack score is equal to or higher than the evade score, then the attack hits, and the attacker can roll for damage.
