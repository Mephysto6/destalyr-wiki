 Welcome to the Destalyr Wiki home page.


Destalyr is a tabletop role-playing game, like Dungeons&Dragons.
The game Destalyr takes place in a medieval fantasy world where magic is common and problems are plenty.
Destalyr is the name of the continent which holds the 6 known races of humanoids (called humans), the rest of the world remains undiscovered.






New ?
Here is our reading order suggestion
1- Lore > geography, history, races
2- Lore > beliefs, elemental magic, runic magic, magic uses
3- Character > magic, stats, creation
4- System > combat, attack, gauges, weapons and armors
5- System > magic explanation, creation, manipulation, ballparks
6- the rest
